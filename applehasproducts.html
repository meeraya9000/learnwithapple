Apple's products

What type of products does apple sell?

Apple sells a wide range of products, including Mac computers, iPhones, iPads, Apple Watches, AirPods, AirTags, Apple TV 4K, and HomePod. They also offer a variety of accessories, such as cases, bands, and chargers, as well as services like Apple Music, Apple TV+, and iCloud . Out of all these products the iphone is the highest selling product making up 50% of apples sales.

Apple's best selling products

The iphone

  • The iphone is one of the biggest phones on the market, with the most recent being the iphone 14.
  • The iphone 14 has a 6.1 inch Super Retina XDR display
  • The iphone 14 is also powered by the A15 bionic chip with a 6-core CPU, 5-core GPU and 16-core Neural Engine.

The ipad

  • The ipad is a touchscreen PC made by apple that runs on an iPadOS system.
  • Apple has four iPad product lines: iPad, iPad mini, iPad Air and Ipad Pro.
  • It has a Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity

Apple Watch

  • The Apple Watch is a line of smartwatches produced by Apple Inc. It incorporates fitness tracking, health-oriented capabilities, and wireless telecommunication, and integrates with iOS and other Apple products and services.
  • 100 Million Apple Users Wear the Apple Watch
  • The Apple Watch Is The Most Smartwatch Sold In The World
